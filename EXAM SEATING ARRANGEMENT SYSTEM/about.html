About Us

Welcome to the Examination Seating Arrangement System, an innovative solution designed to streamline and simplify the process of managing seating arrangements for exams. We are a team of second-year students from the Computer Science and Engineering department, driven by a passion for problem-solving and technology. This project was created as part of our semester's innovative project, where we were said to design a working prototype of an Examination Seating Arrangement system.

Our goal with this project is to help educational institutions reduce the complexity and time involved in organizing exam seating arrangements. By leveraging modern web technologies, we've built a user-friendly platform that provides hassle-free, organized, and flexible seating plans at the click of a button.

This project has allowed us to apply and expand our knowledge in HTML, CSS and JavaScript, while developing a system that addresses practical challenges faced by administrators and faculty.
